            • No Sync Needed

              The unique two-in-one nature of PadFone removes the need to tediously synchronize data between smartphone and tablet - all of your movies, photos and songs are right where you left them.

            • Performance

              With a quad-core CPU and ultra-responsive 65ms touchscreen, the mini keeps up with any hectic lifestyle.

            • Superior Performance

              Powered by the Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ 400 Quad-core CPU (1.4GHz) and 1GB of RAM, PadFone mini 4.3 is smooth and responsive.

            • Long Lasting Battery Life

              PadFone mini 4.3 features up to 12.5 hours of talk time, increased to up to 22 hours when docked into PadFone mini Station. PadFone mini Station's built-in battery also has sufficient capacity to fully recharge the docked PadFone mini once over.

            • Camera

              The 8MP Sony BSI camera with f/2 lens provides fantastic quality pictures, with ASUS PixelMaster low-light or night shooting enhancements included.

            • Low-light

              The ASUS PixelMaster camera can adjust the size of each pixel to ensure the best possible picture quality in any given scenario. For day light photography, PixelMaster creates clear 8-megapixel photos packed with detail. In low light, PixelMaster automatically adjusts the pixel size to increase light sensitivity by up to 400% for great quality photos, without the need for the LED flash.

            • Ultra Burst Shot

              Shoot fast moving images at 16 shots per second and never miss a moment.

            • All Smile

              Adjust expressions so that everyone looks their best.

            • Smart Remove

              Smart remove unwanted objects from the frame.

            • Beautification

              Have some fun and add some Blush, soften skin, perk your cheeks or enhance your eyes.

            • Design

              A compact, transforming design with dual SIM and micro SD slot in one device is perfect for travel.

              Micro-SD slot

            • Compact & Portable

              With a stylish phone design that tapered to just 8.7mm and weighs a mere 105g, PadFone mini 4.3 slips into a pocket just as easily as it slips into the hand.

            • Dual SIM One Device

              PadFone mini 4.3 allows you to have both devices on the go! 7" tablet fits perfectly in your bag whilst also having a 4.3" smartphone that has dual SIM dual standby. This great features provides you worry free lifestyle, without missing any important messages! *Notice: Specifications may differ from country to country.
